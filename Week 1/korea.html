South Korea

  1. In South Korea you can find the fastest internet in the world.
  2. The South Korean flag has a number of symbols: red is Yang, blue is Yin and the four symbols around it represent heaven, earth, water and fire.
  3. Around two-thirds of the land in South Korea is forested. This includes the mainland and some of the more than 3,000 small islands that are located on the country’s southern and western coasts.
  4. The literacy rate is extremely high in South Korea at 98%.
  5. In Korea, a baby is one year old at birth, instead of zero as in most other cultures.
  6. The number ‘4’ is considered to be unlucky.

You should visit South Korea for its beautiful eleven UNESCO World Heritage sites. You can easily visit one of these sites, the Jeju island, by taking the KTX to Busan and then a ferry from there.
